dss-codec

DSS / DS2 In-Browser Demo

Load a `.dss` or `.ds2` file, detect encryption, optionally unlock it with a password, render its waveform, and play the generated WAV directly in the browser. All inspection, decryption, decoding, and WAV generation happen locally on your device with no external upload or processing.

This demo must be served over HTTP. Run npm run demo and open http://127.0.0.1:4173/examples/simple-html/.
